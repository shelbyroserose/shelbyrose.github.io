About Me

Hi, I’m Shelby Rose. I’m a registered nurse, current Yale midwifery student, and a content manager for a reproductive health app. I care deeply about accessible, empathetic care and believe health education can be both accurate and affirming. My path has taken me from hospital bedsides to food distribution tables in Prague — and I’m just getting started.

What I Do

  • Digital Health Content: Content Manager at CARÁ Health — I write and edit maternal health articles that are evidence-based, accessible, and engaging.
  • Clinical Nursing: Experience in OB/GYN clinics, NICU, postpartum units, and school nursing across the U.S. and Europe.
  • Freelance & Future Plans: I’m available for mission-driven freelance content work — and soon, I’ll be launching a digital sex ed course for schools.
